Privacy Policy

How personal information is handled on gmacovei.com.

Last updated: July 10, 2026

What this site collects

gmacovei.com is a personal professional website. It does not currently provide a contact form or sell products. If you contact Gabriel by email or through LinkedIn, the information you provide is handled through those services.

Local preferences

The site stores your light or dark appearance preference in your browser using local storage. This preference is not transmitted to Gabriel.

External links

This website links to RielArt, LinkedIn, email, and other third-party websites. Those services have their own privacy policies and data practices.

Analytics and cookies

No advertising cookies are intentionally set by this static website. If analytics are added in the future, this policy will be updated before those tools are used.

Your questions

For privacy questions, email hello@gmacovei.com.
